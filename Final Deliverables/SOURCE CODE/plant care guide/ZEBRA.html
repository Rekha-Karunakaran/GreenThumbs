ZEBRA PLANT



Zebra Plant has emerald green leaves with dramatic white veins so striking, it is grown for its foliage. Plumes of bright yellow flowers emerging from golden bracts in fall are an added attraction.

CARING TIPS


Temperature:

Warm 65-80°F/18-27°C year-round.


Light:

Bright light but no direct sun. Wrinkled or curled leaves indicate it is getting too much light.


Watering:

Keep soil evenly moist year-round. Dry soil will cause leaves to wilt or drop off.


Soil:

Peat moss based potting mix with added perlite to keep the mix light and free-draining. African violet mix is ideal.


Humidity:

Moderate to high (aim for 50% relative humidity or higher).


Fertilizer:

Feed monthly spring and summer with a water-soluble fertilizer.
