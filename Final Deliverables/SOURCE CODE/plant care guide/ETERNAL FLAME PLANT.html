ETERNAL FLAME PLANT


:This plant is native to Brazil and tropical AmericaThe name ‘Eternal Flame’ comes from the yellow and orange flowers that resemble a flame. They grow on the top of the stems, a little higher than the leaves.

CARING TIPS


Temperature:

It likes humidity and warmth. Not lower than 60ºF (16ºC). It will do well in a room temperature


Light:

A bright room, but no direct sunlight as that can make the color of the leaves fade away


Watering:

Eternal flame likes to have an even amount of water. It is especially important during the summer months, when it is warmer.


Soil:

The best for potting the Calathea Crocata is soil based potting compost.


Humidity:

These need high humidity and plenty of gentle misting
