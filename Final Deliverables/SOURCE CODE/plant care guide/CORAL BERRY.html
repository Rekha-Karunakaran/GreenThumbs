CORAL BERRY


The coral berry is a shrub that grows with a single stem or possibly multiple branches displaying leathery oval shaped leaves, which can be grown outdoors or indoors with the correct conditions.

CARING TIPS


Temperature:

Average warmth is fine of approximately 60-75°F (15-24°C) and no lower than 45°F (8°C).


Light:

A lover of bright light, not direct sunlight (or at least not much)


Watering:

Water when the soil begins losing it’s moisture to the touch, and naturally reduce watering during winter.


Soil:

Most well draining potting mixes will suffice.


Humidity:

Average house humidity is advised. Misting leaves will improve humidity during the summer months.
