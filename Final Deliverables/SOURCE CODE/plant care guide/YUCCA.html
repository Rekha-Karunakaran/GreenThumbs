YUCCA PLANT



Gaining in popularity, the yucca plant makes a beautiful floor accent year-round. Many cultivars are available to choose from. 'Variegata' leaves are broadly edged in white and 'Silver Star' has silvery gray-green leaves.

Watering:

Keep soil moist in spring through fall. In winter, water just enough to keep soil from drying out. Be careful not to overwater, especially in winter. Use a container with drainage holes to prevent soggy soil, which can cause root rot.


Soil:

Soil-based potting mix.


Humidity:

Average indoor (around 40% relative humidity). This desert native is tolerant of dry air.
