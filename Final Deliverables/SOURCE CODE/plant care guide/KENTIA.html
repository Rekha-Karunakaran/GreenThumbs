KENTIA PALM PLANT


COMMON NAME: Kentia, paradise, thatch

The Kentia palm is a fairly easy plant member from the Howea genus to take care of indoors that displays wide leaflets.They look the part sitting within a conservatory, office or hallway and add an exotic look to any room.

CARING TIPS


Temperature:

Room temperatures of around 65°f / 16°c — 75°f / 24°c are ideal. The temperature should not become lower than 55°f / 12.7°c.


Light:

It grows well and maintains it’s health in low light conditions.


Watering:

Having good drainage from the pot is important to prevent root rot. Checking the top soil for moisture (by touch) is the best way to see if they need water. If the top soil is dry in the summer–give him a drink, and in the winter I would advise you to allow some dryness.


Soil:

A fast draining soil mix is best to use.


Humidity:

They do survive well in quite humid conditions naturally.
