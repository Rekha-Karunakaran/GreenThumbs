BARBERTON DAISY-GERBERA JASMESONII


Barberton Daisy is Native to South Africa this species is a perennial in warmer climates but in cooler countries (temperate regions) it’s grown as an annual and flowering pot plant..

CARING TIPS


Temperature:

Average temperatures of 55 – 75°F (13 – 24°C) are suitable


Light:

A lover of direct sunlight and bright windowsills.


Watering:

Best to keep the soil moist at all times. Once the soil becomes slightly dry to the touch provide water straight away.


Soil:

A peat based potting mix with 2 parts peat and 1 part perlite is suggested.


Humidity:

Gerbera’s quite like above average humidity levels, although average is absolutely fine..
