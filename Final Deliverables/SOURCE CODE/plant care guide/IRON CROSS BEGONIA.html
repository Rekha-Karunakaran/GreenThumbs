IRON CROSS BEGONIA


Botanical Name: Begonia masoniana
Iron cross begonia leaves are easily recognizable. Each bright-green puckered leaf is marked with a reddish-brown iron-cross pattern in the center..

Light:

Give your begonia bright, indirect light. You'll get the best leaf color this way. It thrives under fluorescent lights..


Watering:

Water thoroughly, allowing the top 1 in (2.5 cm) of soil to dry out between waterings. Avoid getting water on the leaves because they spot easily and are prone to mildew and other fungus..


Soil:

Peat moss based with added perlite and/or vermiculite for faster drainage. African violet potting mix is ideal..


Humidity:

This fancy-leaf begonia likes 50% relative humidity or higher.
