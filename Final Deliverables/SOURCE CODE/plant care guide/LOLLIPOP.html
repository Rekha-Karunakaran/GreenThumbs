LOLLIPOP PLANT


COMMON NAME: Lollipop/golden shrimp plant

The lollipop plant is a sub-tropical species that produces small cone shaped bract flowers, golden or yellow in color. Taking care of this shrub is fairly easy. It has the basic needs of any plant, although it needs to be pruned regularly to keep it’s size manageable

CARING TIPS


Temperature:

Average room temperatures of around 65°f / 16°c — 75°f are ideal during winter. You can allow the plant some time outdoors during summer, not in direct sun.


Light:

Plenty of daylight, but not direct sunlight.


Watering:

Keep the soil damp and water frequently during summer. Water sparingly winter time.


Soil:

A decent fast draining potting soil mix, will suffice.


Humidity:

The golden shrimp enjoys misting (summertime).


Re-Potting:

Re-pot during spring each year. If not each year, then at-least when the roots start to grow from the pot drainage holes.
