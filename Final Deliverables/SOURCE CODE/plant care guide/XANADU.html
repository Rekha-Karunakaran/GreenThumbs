CARING TIPS


Temperature:

Philodendron Xanadu likes warm temperatures above 55 degrees. Keep them away from drafts and open doors, especially during the winter.


Light:

It prefers medium to bright indirect light.


Watering:

You should be watering your plant until water drains from the pot's drainage holes (houseplants should always be planted in pots with drainage holes).


Soil:

Well-draining soil mix that doesn't retain water for too long.


Humidity:

Will do fine in normal home humidity, but thrives in more humid environments. Mist your plant occasionally for best results.
